Sweet Recipes

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry milk powder
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar

Recipe

  1. In a large bowl, stir together the milk powder, flour, baking powder, and cardamom.
  2. In a large skillet, stir together the sugar, water, rose water and a pinch of cardamom. Set aside.
  3. Fill a large heavy skillet halfway with oil. Heat over medium heat for at least 5 minutes. Knead the dough, and form into about 20 small balls.
  4. Place the balls into the skillet with the syrup. Serve immediately, or chill.

Choclate Burfi

Ingredients

  • Mawa
  • Sugar
  • Coco Powder
  • Ghee or any fats

Recipe

  1. Use the high quality of mawa or khoya for this recipe.
  2. Cook your mawa on lower flame as it gets dry and burnt very easily.
  3. Make sure to set the burfi completely before cutting it or it will get sloppy.
  4. .Cook only till the sugar is completely dissolved.
  5. Smoothen it out and on the top add the chocolate mixture. Cut and enjoy!

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Butter Ghee

Recipe

  1. Place 1 cup cashews (kaju) in a dry grinder or coffee grinder or blender.
  2. Grind the kaju to a smooth powder, making sure you don’t over do the grinding process.
  3. Combine ½ cup sugar and 5 tablespoons water in a thick bottomed pan .
  4. When the sugar completely dissolves in the water, add the cashew powder.
  5. Arrange them on a plate one at a time as you go.

Dal Bati Churma

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chana dal
  • 2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour

Recipe

  1. Rinse the dals well and pressure cook for 5 whistles.
  2. In a mixing bowl – take all the ingredients listed under ‘for bati’, mix well and knead it together to form a stiff dough.
  3. In a mixing bowl -take wheat flour, rava and ghee, add water to form a stiff dough.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them using your palms.
